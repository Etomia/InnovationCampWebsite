What is AIDE?

AIDE is an artificial intelligence that analyzes data automatically, and through algorithms / machine learning, it can present specific solutions for specific regions to representatives from Redd Barna, so that they can move quickly and in a cost-effectively way implement measures to reduce absenteeism.

Why do you need AIDE?

AIDE is the best AI capable of doing preventive mesures. By analyzing data on school attendence, AIDE cross-analyse the data with other broader data (geography, weather conditions, war conditions, illness, etc.). From this, AIDE can find the reason behind trends of absence and present Redd Barna an optimal solution to prevent further absence. It will also be able to warn about drought, food shortages and present Redd Barna with optimalized preventative measures. Over time, AIDE will learn how and why absence happens, and the preventative measures will come earlier and earlier. Preventive work is what we want to emphasize through AIDE and it is the best tool to resolve major world problems.

Principles of Digital Development

Adapt, improve, expand. A major criteria when designing AIDE was that it had be in accordance with the Principals of Digital Development. We designed AIDE to be scalable worldwide and making sure that it can be adapted to different regions. The data is universal, encrypted and expandable. It is built to last and will be updated with the latest technology to ensure optimal performance. AIDE will share the data with Redd Barna, nations and all kinds of welfare organizations.
